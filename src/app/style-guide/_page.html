06

Principles

01

One action per view

Do give each screen a single neon CTA. Don't stack two shimmer buttons — the neon loses its meaning.

02

Serif thinks, sans speaks

Do use light Caslon for the ideas (headings). Don't set body or UI in the serif, and never italicize a heading.

03

Neon is sacred

Do keep #D0FF71 for sign-up. Don't use it for borders, text, or decoration — use periwinkle for accents.

04

Warm, never stark

Do build on cream with ink text. Don't use pure white pages or pure #000 text — it reads cold and off-brand.

05

Round everything

Do round corners (100 / 22 / 16 / 14). Don't ship a hard-cornered card, button, or image band.

06

Pixel = label, not prose

Do use Geist Pixel for eyebrows, tags, and the wordmark. Don't set sentences or paragraphs in it.
