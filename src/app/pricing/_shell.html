How it works

One connection today, every answer from here on.

01

Connect once

One Google OAuth grant links every client's data to ARLO — GA4, Google Ads, Search Console, Meta, Shopify and more. Set it once, at the workspace level.

02

Ask in plain language

Paste your MCP URL into Claude Desktop and ask about any client, any platform. No exports, no dashboards, no tab-hopping — just the answer.

03

Reporting that runs itself

Monday mornings stop being a scramble. Every client is covered, every answer is a sentence away, and your hours go to strategy instead of screenshots.
