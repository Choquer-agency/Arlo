# Arlo — Keyword Research & Opportunity Map

**Domain:** https://askarlo.app
**Product:** MCP connector that plugs Claude into an agency's marketing accounts (GA4, Search Console, Google Ads, Meta, YouTube, Shopify) for live conversational reporting. No exports, no dashboards, no warehouse. Priced per business tracked.
**Direct competitors:** Windsor.ai, Supermetrics (adjacent: Funnel.io, Whatagraph, AgencyAnalytics, Looker Studio)
**Markets:** United States (primary) + Canada (secondary)
**Data source:** SE Ranking live API (US + CA databases) — volume, keyword difficulty (KD 0–100), CPC, intent
**Date pulled:** 2026-07-04
**Intent codes:** I = Informational, N = Navigational, C = Commercial, T = Transactional, L = Local

> All volume / KD / CPC figures below are **live SE Ranking data** unless explicitly labeled *(estimate)*. Terms SE Ranking returned as "no data" are listed at the bottom as negligible/zero-volume — they were **not** assigned invented numbers.

---

## TL;DR — the beachhead

A brand-new domain cannot touch the head MCP terms ("mcp server" KD 88, "mcp servers" KD 92, "model context protocol" KD 95) for 12+ months. But there is a genuine **low-KD, commercially-warm cluster** that is (a) rankable in 3–6 months and (b) exploding in volume as MCP adoption compounds:

- **Integration/how-to MCP terms** — `google ads mcp` (KD 18, 590/mo, +8x YoY), `google analytics mcp` (KD 21, 390/mo, +39x YoY), `claude mcp server` (KD 21, 810/mo) — low difficulty, transactional-adjacent, and on a steep growth curve. **This is Arlo's single best wedge.**
- **Alternative/comparison terms** — `supermetrics alternative` (KD 16, 170/mo, **CPC $33.76**), `alternative to supermetrics` (KD 9), `supermetrics competitors` (KD 16) — tiny volume but the highest purchase intent and CPC on the board.
- **Agency-reporting persona terms** — `marketing agency reporting` (KD 14, 170/mo, CPC $20.90), `seo agency reporting software` (KD 16, 260/mo), `client reporting tool` (KD 18, 140/mo) — the buyer's own language.

Build authority here first, then climb toward the KD 50–70 mid terms (`mcp integration`, `claude mcp`, `conversational analytics`), and only later contest the KD 85+ heads.

---

## Priority scoring key

- **P1 — Attack now (0–3 mo):** KD ≤ 25 AND commercial/transactional or high-intent integration. Realistically rankable for a new domain; these get dedicated pages.
- **P2 — Near-term (3–6 mo):** KD 26–45, or low-KD but very low volume (supporting/long-tail). Cluster support pages + blog.
- **P3 — Build toward (6–12 mo+):** KD > 45 head terms. Pillar content, earn links, revisit once domain authority exists.
